About us

This organisation is founded by ten students of VNIT Nagpur who wants to do something for their society and country. We came to know that most of the waste produced in our locality goes to dumpyard and is of no utility. This gave us an idea that we can create a platform where user can easily earn money by selling scrap.

What we do?

Our organisation works as a mediator between scrap dealer and scrap producer. Through out site user can get the optimum price for its scrap rather than throwing it as a waste. We have contacts amongst maximum dealers who recycle those scrap. We have also a branch which sells the recycled items produced by these tdealers

How does this work?

Firstly the user require to log in on our site , there he can select the items which he wants to sell(currently we are dealing with only few items, in future the list of items can be extended)and the quantity. The minimum value of hundred rupees is required for the order to proceed. The user is required to to enter the address and upload the photo of the items. As soon as we get the order we contact to the scrap vendor. He collects the scrap from the customer and user gets the money on spot.

Legal and financial

Our aim is to turn waste into new raw material whenever possible and keep it in circulation as long as possible. We offer recycling services for several materials such as plastics, metals, clothes, newapapers, etc. We sell these recycled items on our site and thats how we make money.

Contributing to environment

Recycling is necessary if we want to protect this world for our prospect generations. We make new products fron the waste and old unused products. By reusing and not throwing your old prodicts you are acryally recycling. Recycling is significant to natural environment as well as human beings.
